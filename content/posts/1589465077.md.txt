Walmart traffic grew by 55% compared to 15% for Amazon 

From Marketplace Pulse on 2020 05 14
> Web traffic to Walmart increased by 55% in April compared to February, before the pandemic started. By comparison, both eBay and Amazon saw a 15% growth. Additionally, on May 13th, Walmart became the number one most-downloaded app in the shopping category on the iPhone App Store. On April 11th, Walmart merged its Grocery app into the main app.
