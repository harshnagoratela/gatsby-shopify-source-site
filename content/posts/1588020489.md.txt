Cash is on the way out - even for in-person transactions. 

It’s beneficial for health reasons - and causes less friction in long run. Not likely to go back. 

From The Ledger – Fortune on 2020 04 27
> Reports from customers, however, suggest the cash ban may be more extensive. Attorney Daniel Nazer told Fortune that cashiers at a Whole Foods on Ocean Avenue in San Francisco told shoppers last week that stores would not accept cash at all beginning on Monday.
