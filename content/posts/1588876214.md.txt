From The Ledger – Fortune on 2020 05 07
> Though it already offers some encryption, Zoom says the Keybase acquisition will help it completely redesign how it encrypts calls. For instance, in Zoom’s current design, the codes used to encrypt a secure call are generated by Zoom’s own servers, which could compromise security if hackers gained access to them. Zoom said it will now develop an option for users to generate these encryption keys on their own devices.
