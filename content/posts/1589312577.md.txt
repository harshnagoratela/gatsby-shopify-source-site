Ecommerce sales grew 49% between March and April. 

“Online grocery helped drive the increase in sales, with a 110% boost in daily sales between March and April”

From eCommerce – TechCrunch on 2020 05 12
> U.S. e-commerce jumped 49% in April, compared to the baseline period in early March before shelter-in-place restrictions went into effect. Online grocery helped drive the increase in sales, with a 110% boost in daily sales between March and April. Meanwhile, electronic sales were up 58% and book sales have doubled.
