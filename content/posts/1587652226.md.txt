Some Amazon sellers are running out of inventory at FBA warehoues and turning to alternative 3pl solutions. 

From Marketplace Pulse on 2020 04 23
> Furthermore, across hundreds of thousands of active Amazon sellers in the U.S. marketplace, the share has also decreased from 55% to 50%. Most of the sellers have adapted to fulfill orders from their own warehouse, moved some to an alternative third-party logistics (3PL) warehouse, or are waiting for FBA to restore operations hoping their supplies there last.
